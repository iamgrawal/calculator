# Privacy Policy for Calculator

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Calculator Android application (hereinafter referred to as "Calculator") respects your privacy. Calculator is a basic calculator and currency converter application designed for ease of use and functionality.

## Data Collection and Storage

Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal data, cookies, or any other information about its users. All calculations and conversions are performed locally on your device.

## No Cookies

We do not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies in our application.

## No Personal Data

Calculator does not require or collect any form of personal data from its users. The application functions offline without any need for an internet connection, ensuring your data remains private and secure on your device.

## Currency Converter Functionality

The currency converter feature operates without the need to collect personal data. Exchange rates are updated at regular intervals when the device is connected to the internet, but no personal information is collected during this process.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
